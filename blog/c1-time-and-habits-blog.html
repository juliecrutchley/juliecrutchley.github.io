Time and Habit Mangagement

Posted on Saturday, 4 March 2017

Which timebox techniques did you try?

Meditation - I've been itching to try this for some time now, so this was a great opportunity to start. I downloaded the Headspace app and completed day one. It might only have been 10 minutes of sitting on my bed,but I really dug it. With daily prompts to complete more, this could be the start of a great thing for me!

I also tried Pomodoro - this technique was completely new to me, but man, it makes a lot of sense.

What did you find interesting?

There are so many techniques to improve the way we manage our time and create good habits. Something that really stood out to me was this post by George Dvorsky in relation to the science behind meditation, and in particular, this statement:

"instead of trying to hack into the mind of God, Buddhists are trying to hack into their own mind to harness it under control".

If we're spending all day trying to 'hack' into computers effectively, surely it would help to set aside a little time each day to hack into our own minds!

What worked for you?

Although I've only completed one mini-session, meditation really did seem to clear and refocus my mind.

Which will you experiment further with?

Definitely meditation and pomodoro. I can see a trip to Kmart in the near future to purchase a tomato kitchen timer (totally necessary right?) - any excuse to go to Kmart... I'd also like to look further into the flow technique, perhaps I can complete one pomodoro per week on flow research.
