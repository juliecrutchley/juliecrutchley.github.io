What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'?

I learned that often I am not 100% focussed on listening to others and fully understanding what they are trying to get across to me. Focussing my full attention is now something I am going to be conscious of on a daily basis.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I will attempt to use all three techniques of listening, looping and dipping as I think elements of all are required for the full effect.
