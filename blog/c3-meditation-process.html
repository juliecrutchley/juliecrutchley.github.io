How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the redesign and build?

Being a perfectionist, this was a concept I had to keep reinforcing with the redesign project.

What did you think about meditation before reading Chapter 2?

I was really interested in meditation before starting to read the book, and was completely hooked after reading Chapter 1. I knew it had calming benefits, but I actually was very unaware of some of the more tangible physical benefits.

What new things have you learned about meditation?

I have learned much more about the possible mental and physical benefits from meditation. The example of psoriasis sufferers seeing improvement with meditation is incredible.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

The technique of keeping your eyes half closed stood out to me and is something that I am going to try!
